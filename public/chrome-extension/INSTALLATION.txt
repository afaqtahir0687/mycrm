CHROME EXTENSION INSTALLATION GUIDE
================================================================================

STEP 1: Open Chrome Extensions Page
------------------------------------
1. Open Google Chrome browser
2. Type in address bar: chrome://extensions/
3. Press Enter

STEP 2: Enable Developer Mode
------------------------------
1. Look for "Developer mode" toggle in top-right corner
2. Turn it ON (it will turn blue)

STEP 3: Load the Extension
---------------------------
1. Click the "Load unpacked" button (top-left)
2. Navigate to: C:\xampp\htdocs\crm\public\chrome-extension
3. Click "Select Folder"

================================================================================
HOW TO USE THE EXTENSION
================================================================================

1. Open your social media account (Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.) in a Chrome tab
2. Navigate to the page with data you want:
   - Facebook: Friends list, Group members page
   - LinkedIn: Connections page, Search results
   - Instagram: Followers/Following page
   - Twitter: Followers/Following page
   - TikTok: Followers/Following page

3. Click the CRM Data Scraper extension icon
4. Select the source (Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.)
5. Click "Extract Data from Current Tab"
6. Wait for extraction to complete
7. Review the extracted data preview
8. Click "Send to CRM" to import the data

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Make sure your CRM is running at: http://localhost/crm
- The extension only extracts data from the currently active tab
- All data stays on your computer until you send it to CRM
- Ensure compliance with platform Terms of Service
